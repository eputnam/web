THE BAND

Bells and Hunters was formed in 2008 by Kelly Ann Beavers and Keith Fischer over a mutual love of creativity, song writing, and Jeff Buckley. In the years since, the band has distinguished itself with an eclectic sound that mixes elements of American folk, blues, and 90's rock behind Kelly and Keith's soulful vocals. In 2010 the band recorded their debut EP, "The Static Sea," and in 2013 released the critically acclaimed album "Weddings and Funerals". Currently, the band continues to perform live - including notable 2014 performances at Gypsy Sally's (Washington DC), Spike Hill (Brooklyn, NY), and the Melonfest Music Festival (Berea, KY) - and is currently recording their 3rd album set for release in 2015.
